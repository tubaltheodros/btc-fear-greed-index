Why Measure Fear and Greed?

The crypto market behavior is highly emotional. Greedy investors can inflate the market, while fear can drive irrational sell-offs. With this index, we aim to help you make informed decisions and avoid emotional overreactions.

  • Extreme fear: May signal a buying opportunity.
  • Extreme greed: May indicate the market is overheated.

Data Sources
