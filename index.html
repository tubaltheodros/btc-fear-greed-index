Data Sources

I gathered this data from four main sources to create a comprehensive Bitcoin Fear and Greed Index. The algorithm analyzes sentiment on Reddit discussions, calculates Bitcoin's 24-hour price volatility patterns, and incorporates data from established sentiment indicators. These diverse inputs are weighted and combined through a custom Python algorithm to produce a single, robust metric that captures market sentiment more accurately than any individual source could provide.

  • Market Volatility Analysis (40%): Quantifies Bitcoin's recent price movements against historical volatility patterns to identify periods of market uncertainty or confidence.
  • Alternative.me Sentiment Index (30%): Incorporates established sentiment metrics that analyze discussions across multiple social platforms and forums to detect shifting market psychology.
  • Reddit Sentiment Analysis (20%): Uses natural language processing to evaluate the tone, frequency, and engagement of Bitcoin-related discussions on the platform where cryptocurrency enthusiasts gather.
